## Core Claims to Validate

- Ranking metrics and deployment-threshold behavior can diverge sharply under severe imbalance.
- HDDT-family models can offer operational advantages at default thresholds on selected datasets.
- Fixed-threshold sweeps are essential for interpreting imbalance behavior beyond AUROC/AP.
